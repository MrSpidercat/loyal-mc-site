Frequently Asked Questions


  1. Question: Do I need a PayPal account to purchase a rank?

  2. Question: How do I know this is safe?
    Answer: All Purchases are secured by PayPal and Loyal MC doesn't receive your information.


  3. Question: Do the ranks make the server P2W? (Pay to Win)
    Answer: Absolutely not! We strive to make sure both regular players and our supporters stay on a equal playing field.


  4. Question: How long will it take for me to receive my rank?
    Answer: It could take up to 24 hours but you will most likely get it no more than 2 hours from purchase.


  5. Question: What do you do with the money you make from these ranks?
    Answer: All the money donated goes back into the server and helps us continue to keep it running.
